Where Am I?

The first chapter talks about determining where you are. It makes sense to me that you can't get to where you want to be in life unless you know where you are starting. The starting point is divided into 4 categories. They are:

  • Health
  • Work
  • Play
  • Love
    • Each category is assesed by a dashboard

    Compass Building

    WorkView

    I work to improve my life and the lives of others. I believe that means making things that helps to get thing done or does things. That could be physical or virtual things. Physcical things like installing a door. Virtual things like an automation program. I work to make enoguh money to pay for things I can't or don't want to do. I think work should be challenging and inspire growth (mental and physical)
